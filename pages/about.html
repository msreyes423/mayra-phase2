What is Aigoo?

Aigoo (or aigo) translates to OMG from Korean but mostly is just used as a general term to express frustration. It is an expression that you'll hear a lot in Kdramas (often comically) when a character is stressed, shocked, in pain, tired etc. so I thought it was only fitting to name the site that as well. Trust me you'll have many "aigoo" moments yourself as you're watching. Here you'll find Kdrama reviews, news and chatter. Welcome to Aigoo!
