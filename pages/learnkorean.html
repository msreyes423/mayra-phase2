Start Learning Korean Today!

Maybe you're just interested in learning languages or maybe you wanna learn so you can watch Kdramas without reading the subtitles. (I sure wish I could lol.) Either way, here's a list of some suggested sites to get you started:

Memrise

This is a great place to start. This site is completely free and offers lessons in multiple languages. It relies on its community of learners to teach and improve language for everyone.

Duolingo

Learn Korean in 5 miniutes a day with game-like lessons. Bite-sized lessons that are fun, effective and 100% free. There's even an app too!

Rocket Languages

This site has extensive paid lessons but offers many free lessons on common phrases and words you'd need when travelling. They even take it a step further with a cool tool that tests what you have learn in a few challenging ways. "Hear It Say It!" prompts you to record a spoken phrase to see how your pronunciation compares. There's also the option to test you writing skills and your translation skills as well.

KoreanClass101

Offers a paid service but has a lot of free material available as well. They offer free podcast lessons as well as hours of free Youtube content.

How to Study Korean

This site is basically a free digital course book with everything you need to know. It's laid out in a way that each lesson builds onto the next and it's easy to follow. It starts with the alphabet and builds from there. It aims to truly teach you how to speak Korean and not just help you learn some helpful words and phrases.
